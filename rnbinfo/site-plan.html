Overview

Purpose

Think about your favorite kind of music. A lot of people will say pop, lots of people will say rap, lots of people in Idaho would say country too. But talking about the first two, pop and rap, a lot of the songs that are coming out now are kind of blurring the line between those two genres. Its formed a new genre, called RnB. "Rhythm and beats". I plan on making an informational website about the different artists and songs, so that people can understand this newgenre better, and even get more interested!

Audience

Everyone wanting to break into the musical genre of RnB!
